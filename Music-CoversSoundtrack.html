Did you know?

When the crew was filming this scene, the cameramen sat on helicopters to get the overhead shot. Not only that, but some of the grass was also a bit yellow so they were colored in green electronically and the birch tree trunks you see were set props carried all the way up to the hills by the crew.

8. Edelweiss

Did you know?

Though many believe “Edelweiss” to be an authentic Austrian folk song, it was actually the last song Rodgers and Hammerstein ever jointly wrote before Hammerstein died in August 1960. Supposedly, he had known he was dying while writing this song, making it all the more emotional.
